about me

I remember being a little kid and always having a creative vision in my head. Being introduced to paper, color pencils, and markers was the moment I first started to truly feel alive. Sometimes I would draw for hours, getting enveloped in each of my pieces. My teachers would pull me out of class to design banners, folders or other side projects they needed assistance with. When I reached my 20's I knew I needed to release my art unto the world. I quickly discovered the Indie Market, where I'd pour my heart & soul into prints, keychains, clothing, canvases, and more. This led to commissions and I was able to create logos, designs and even tattoos.
